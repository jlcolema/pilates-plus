The mind housed in a beautiful body possesses a glorious sense of power.

You've probably come across Pilates in various places over the past several years. There is a reason for that. In addition to developing strength and flexibility, Pilates focuses on quality not quantity and is designed to work the deepest muscles of the body creating a strong core.

In Pilates, we work the “powerhouse” which comprises the abdominal muscles, hips, lower back muscles and gluteals. Practicing Pilates will allow you to move with control from your center with concentration and precision while using proper breathing to create a natural flow from one exercise to the next. All of our movement is done in harmony with our minds. Thus, I call Pilates the intelligent exercise!

Mimi Hevel, is BASI certified on all Pilates equipment. She hopes to see you in the studio soon to experience the great benefits of Pilates!
